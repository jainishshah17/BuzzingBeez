About BuzzingBeez

BuzzingBeez is a small home daycare in Castro Valley built around one idea: children thrive when they feel known, safe, and genuinely celebrated. We combine predictable routines with room for curiosity—so families get dependable care and kids get a childhood that feels like childhood.

Our mission

To give every child in our care a warm home base for learning: where emotions are coached, questions are welcome, movement and creativity are part of every week, and families always know what their child’s day looked like.

What guides us every day

Whole-child growth

We look at social skills, confidence, language, and independence together—not as separate checklists.

Honest partnership

We share how the day went, listen to your routines at home, and align on goals so care feels continuous, not compartmentalized.

Safety & trust

A licensed home setting, careful supervision, CPR/First Aid readiness, and clear expectations from day one.

Your child’s first weeks

New children get a gentle ramp: predictable hellos and goodbyes, extra check-ins with parents, and activities that build familiarity before we stretch into bigger group moments. We move at your child’s pace—not a one-size-fits-all calendar.

Meet our founder

Pratiksha leads BuzzingBeez with hands-on teaching experience, a background in structured problem-solving from her prior career, and the lived perspective of raising her own children. Her personal story and photos live on our home page—we’d love for you to read it when you’re deciding if we’re the right fit.
