Common questions

What ages do you serve?
We care for children ages 1–6. Program details and the daily schedule are on our Programs page.
What are your hours?
Exact drop-off and pick-up windows depend on enrollment and are shared during your tour so expectations match your family’s schedule.
What should my child bring?
Typically: diapers and wipes if needed, a change of clothes, indoor shoes or socks, a water bottle, and any small comfort item for rest. We’ll confirm a tailored list before the first day.
How do I enroll?
Start with a tour, then we walk through paperwork and a start date together. See How enrollment works on the Programs page for an overview.
