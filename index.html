A family-centered note from our founder

Meet Pratiksha

Hi, I’m Pratiksha 👋

I’m the founder of BuzzingBeez Home Daycare, a mother of two boys, and a passionate educator who believes early childhood should be filled with love, learning, and joyful experiences.

Before entering Early Childhood Education, I worked as a GIS Technician with companies including Meta and Apple. After becoming a mother, I chose to step away from the corporate world to spend more meaningful time with my children. That journey inspired me to follow my passion for working with young children and eventually become a Preschool Teacher.

BuzzingBeez was created by combining my experience as a mother, educator, and caregiver into a warm, safe, and nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and feel at home.

At BuzzingBeez, we focus on play-based learning, preschool readiness, healthy home-cooked meals, creative activities, and even Bollywood dance & movement classes designed to build confidence, creativity, and self-expression 💃🐝

I look forward to welcoming your family into ours ❤️

“BuzzingBeez was born from my desire to create the kind of nurturing, joyful environment I wanted for my own children.”
